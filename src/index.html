The Online Database for ABS Vessel Records

ABS RecordⓇ®, the online database for ABS vessel records, provides a listing of all vessels and offshore installations cur- rently in class with ABS, including appropriate class notations. Each listing contains the principal particulars of the vessel or installation and information on the machinery, hull, capacities, builder, owner and ABS survey particulars. The data- base also includes listings for vessels with load line statutory certificates that are not ABS classed plus information on stat- utory survey status to the extent authorized by the ship's flag administration.

Class suspensions, withdrawals and re-instatements are posted as they occur. Postings are done at the same time as ABS Survey Status is updated.
